About Us!

The Trinibarjam Election Commission is an autonomous constitutional authority responsible
for administering election processes in Trinidad and Tobago. The body administers elections throughout the country
and the offices of the President and Vice President in the country. The Election Commission operates
under the authority of Constitution per Article 324, and subsequently enacted Representation of the People Act.
